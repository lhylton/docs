The Reports tab is where your research becomes shareable. Strella's AI Chat can generate a complete, custom report drawn from your sessions, themes, and saved insights — in the format and focus you define. Once you've shaped it to your liking, share it with stakeholders via a simple web link, no export or formatting required.

## Create a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Go to **Results > Reports** in your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create a report">
    Select **Create a report** to launch the AI Chat interface in report-creation mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your report">
    Type a description of the report you want. Be as specific as you like — you can specify the audience, the focus areas, the format, or the key questions you want it to answer. For example:

    > "Create an executive summary focused on usability pain points, with key quotes and a recommendation section."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the generated report">
    Strella's AI searches across your project's sessions, themes, and participant responses to assemble a tailored report. It will appear in the chat interface for you to review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also launch report creation from anywhere in your project. Open the **AI Chat** tab and toggle **Create a report** on — then describe your report in the same way.
</Tip>

## Modify a report

After the initial report is generated, continue working with AI Chat to refine it iteratively.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the draft">
    Read through the generated report and note anything you want to change — a section that needs more depth, a focus area to add, or content to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send follow-up prompts">
    Type your refinements directly in the chat. Examples:

    > "Add a section on participant demographics."

    > "Make the recommendations more specific."

    > "Shorten the methodology section."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterate until it's ready">
    Continue refining with as many prompts as you need. Each response updates the report based on your latest instruction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AI Chat always draws from your actual project data when generating or modifying reports. Every claim and quote in the report is grounded in what your participants said.
</Note>

## Share a report

Once your report is ready to go, share it with stakeholders in a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Save the report">
    Click **Save** to store the report in your project's Reports tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the saved report">
    Navigate to **Results > Reports** and click to open the report you just saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Share settings">
    Click **Share** in the report view to open sharing options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share to web">
    Select **Share to web** to generate a publicly accessible link to your report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy and distribute the link">
    Copy the generated link and send it to any stakeholders who need access — they can view the report in a browser without needing a Strella account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The "Share to web" link is publicly accessible to anyone with the URL. Only share it with stakeholders who are authorized to view the research findings.
</Warning>
