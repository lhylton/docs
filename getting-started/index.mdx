Strella is an AI-moderated user research platform that lets you run in-depth participant interviews at scale — without a human moderator in the room. Whether you're validating a new concept, running usability tests, or capturing real-world behavior, Strella guides participants through your question guide, probes for depth, and surfaces insights automatically.

This section walks you through the three essentials every researcher needs before going live: setting up your research study, fine-tuning your project settings, and choosing the right interview format for your goals.
