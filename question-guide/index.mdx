The Question Guide is the heart of every Strella interview. It defines what the AI moderator asks, what media participants see, and how the conversation adapts in real time based on each participant's answers. Whether you're starting from scratch or generating a guide with AI, this section walks you through every step of building an interview that collects the insights you need.

    icon="code-branch"

    Show specific sections only to the participants who qualify—based on screener responses or answers given during the interview itself.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Randomization"
    icon="shuffle"
    href="/question-guide/randomization"
  >
    Reduce order bias when testing multiple concepts by assigning participants to one variant at random, or shuffling all variants into a different sequence for each person.
  </Card>

    Take the interview yourself to catch any issues, then publish to push your changes live and generate a shareable link for self-recruited participants.
