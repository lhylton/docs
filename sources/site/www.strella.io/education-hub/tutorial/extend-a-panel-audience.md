## Audience Extension

When you checkout with a panel order in Strella, sometimes you need to add more participants than you originally planned. The Audience Extension feature lets you do that easily in just a few clicks.

**How It Works 
**

1. Click into the Recruit tab and navigate to Orders
2. Click the three-button dropdown on the right-hand side of your panel order and hit "View"
3. A new screen will pop up showing your order details
4. Locate the "Add participants" button
5. Add the number of additional interviews you'd like to collect (max of 20 at a time)
6. Check out with your corporate card or via invoice!
