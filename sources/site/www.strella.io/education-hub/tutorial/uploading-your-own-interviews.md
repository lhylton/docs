How-to Guide

This tutorial shows you how to bring off-platform or human-moderated interviews into Strella. Learn how to upload your own sessions so they can be included in the AI synthesis. This helps keep all your research in one place for easier analysis and insight generation.

## Uploading Interviews

If you decided to conduct some interviews via human moderation / off platform, but would still like to include the sessions in the synthesis, you can upload your own interviews to the platform. There are two situations where you may want to upload interviews:

1. **Brand new project with interviews being conducted fully off platform**: If you are uploading your own interviews into a brand new research study on the Strella platform, it is important to note that you will need to upload and publish your question guide to ensure the synthesis features work before uploading the interviews. Then you can upload your interviews into the Strella platform using the instructions below.
2. **Existing project with some AI-moderated interviews**: If you are uploading interviews into an already existing project with a question guide already built. please ensure the question guide used for the AI-moderated interviews and human moderated interviews is the same in order for the synthesis features to work most accurately.

In order to upload interviews into the Strella platform, use the following steps:

- Select 'Upload' in the top right corner of the sessions tab
- Drop files or click to upload the videos in the 'Upload' tab and click 'Upload'
- Wait for the videos to process, and they will be added to the sessions tab
