# Previewing and Publishing Your Guide

This tutorial walks you through how to preview your AI-moderated interview, so you can experience it just like a participant and make any final edits. You’ll also learn how to publish your guide and generate a shareable interview link to start collecting responses.

## Previewing your question guide / interview

Once your question guide is complete, it’s time to try it out in action! The next step will be to preview your question guide - this means that you’ll be taking the AI-moderated interview yourself to test it out and see if any revisions to the question guide are needed.

To preview your interview, you can select the 3 dots beside the Publish button in the top left corner and select 'Preview'.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68caf2cf3dc86ed94f27e409_Screenshot%202025-09-17%20at%201.40.59%E2%80%AFPM.png)

A few areas to watch-out for during the preview:

- Does the section order and question order flow in a way that feels natural?
- Does the media show up on screen before the task is read out loud by the AI moderator?
- Do the links to any external websites work?
- Are you asking dynamic follow-ups on the right questions?

Once you have taken the preview in full and noted down any areas that need to be revised, head back to the question guide and make any required changes.

## Publishing your question guide / interview

When you feel like your question guide is ready to be used in real interviews, you can publish the question guide by clicking “Publish” in the top right-hand corner. This pushes through any changes you’ve made to the live interview link, which you can then share with participants.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68caf33dd47c17ea12ff8318_Screenshot%202025-09-17%20at%201.43.07%E2%80%AFPM.png)

If you are recruiting any of your own participants to take the interview, you can use the link generated when you click “Publish” to email participants the interview link.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68caf36d1ba826023acabd4f_Screenshot%202025-09-17%20at%201.44.00%E2%80%AFPM.png)
