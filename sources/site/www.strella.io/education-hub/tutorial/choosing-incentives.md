How-to Guide

This tutorial helps you determine the right incentive amount based on your study type, target audience, and time commitment. Learn how to set fair and motivating incentives that encourage participation and respect your research budget.

## Choosing Incentives

The amount we recommend you pay depends on the type of research study, audience, and time commitment required. Below is some guidance on how to choose your incentive:

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/6866d38d659b41c8dff57f1b_Screenshot%202025-07-03%20150108.png)

**Tip:** Try adjusting incentives for better participation

- Increase pay for **senior-level professionals** or **high-demand skills**
- **Complex or sensitive topics** may require higher incentives
- If recruitment is slow, **raising the per-minute rate** can speed up participation
