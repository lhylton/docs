How-to Guide

This tutorial shows you how to fine-tune your highlight reels by adjusting the transcript snippets included or reordering clips for better flow. Learn how to craft polished, impactful reels that clearly communicate your key insights.

## Editing Highlight Reels

If you are watching through highlight reels and decide that you want to make revisions, there are a few edits you can make.

### Adjust the session transcript

Maybe you're reviewing the session clip and feel like adding more context would bring more value to it. To add more of the transcript to the clip shown in the highlight reel, click ‘View session’.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/6866eff2ae2070b0c7e6773c_Screenshot%202025-07-03%20170148.png)

This will bring you to the part of the transcript where the insight was discussed, you can modify the part of the transcript selected by clicking the blue start or end bars and dragging it to include more or less of the transcript.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/6866f0cf8fa93714ac4ddcb0_Screenshot%202025-07-03%20170610.png)

‍

### Reorder the clips in the highlight reel

Maybe you're watching through the highlight reel and you feel like one specific clip explains the insight perfectly, so you want to bring that clip to the beginning of the highlight reel. To reorder the videos shown in the highlight, drag and drop the note on the right side of the page. By reordering the notes, the order of the videos in the highlight reel will update to match them

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/6866f13ebdafd749f12739ed_Screenshot%202025-07-03%20170801.png)
