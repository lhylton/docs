How-to Guide

This tutorial walks you through exploring your interviews in the Sessions tab. Learn how to view full recordings, use AI-generated notes and summaries, read through transcripts, and review participant screener responses - all in one place to help you stay organized and uncover participant-specific insights faster.

## Navigating the Sessions Tab

Once participants have completed their interviews, the sessions will appear in the ‘Sessions’ tab in the ‘Results’ page.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/6866d652e55b796c8ec7b572_Screenshot%202025-07-03%20151308.png)

When you click into an individual session, you will be able to do the following:

- Watch the session in full
- Read through the session summary
- Read through the entire transcript
- Read through AI-generated notes to get up to speed on the session quickly
- Edit or add new session notes / highlight reels
- See participant screener information
