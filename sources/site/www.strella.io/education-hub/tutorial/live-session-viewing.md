## Live Session Viewing

Researchers can view sessions in real-time in order to mitigate any confusion during the interview, get ahead of technical tasks, etc. and ensure a smooth process overall.

**How It Works**

1. After your project is in field, navigate to the Results tab and click into Sessions
2. If any participants are recording at that time, you will see their session with a label that says "Recording" over it
3. Click into the session and you will see their interview in real-time!
