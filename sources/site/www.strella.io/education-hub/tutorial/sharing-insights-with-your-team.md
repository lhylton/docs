How-to Guide

This tutorial walks you through how to share your insights and highlight reels with teammates - whether or not they have access to Strella. Learn how to create shareable links or download reels so your findings can be easily shared, discussed, and acted on across your organization.

## Sharing Insights With Your Team

To share insights with your team, even if they do not have access to the Strella platform, you can share highlight reels by creating a shareable link or downloading the highlight reel.

To share highlight reels using a shareable link, click on the insight you want to share in the Saved tab. Select the 3 dots in the top right corner of the highlight reel, click 'Create shareable link', and then click 'Copy to clipboard'.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68701a8479b02ac96c7af4d0_Screenshot%202025-07-10%20155317.png)

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68701a98802514044fb9fcca_Screenshot%202025-07-10%20155416.png)

To share highlight reels by downloading the highlight reel, click on the insight you want to share in the Saved tab. Select the 3 dots in the top right corner of the highlight reel, click 'Download Reel'. You'll receive an email when the highlight reel has processed with a link to the downloaded reel.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66d9ac37e276b11648312b1f/68701b15e4d9f45474939cb0_Screenshot%202025-07-10%20155621.png)
