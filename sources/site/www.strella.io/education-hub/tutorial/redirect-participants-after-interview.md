# Redirect Participants After Interview

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/66c895d91c6ea7b93745b98c/6823113946a49b822d6d8add_clock-tutorial.svg)

3

minutes

Tutorial

How-to Guide

Send participants to an external completion page after they click Finish Call by setting your project's Return-To Location.

Use this when you want to redirect participants to a specific URL immediately after they complete an interview.

## **What This Does**

When a participant finishes their call, Strella redirects them to the URL you set in **Project > Settings > Interview setup > Redirect to URL**.

## **When to Use It**

Use post-interview redirects when you need to:

- Send participants to an external completion page.
- Route participants to a specific follow-up experience.
- Keep completion behavior consistent across a project.

## **How to Set Up Return-To Location**

1. Open your project and go to **Settings > Interview setup**‍
2. Find **Redirect to URL**
3. Enter a full URL, including `https://`
4. Save your changes.

Redirect to URL Example: `https://customer-redirect-url.com/call-completed`

## **End-to-End Example**

1. Share your Strella study link with participants: `https://app.strella.io/onboard/abcd1234`
2. Participant completes interview
3. Strella redirects participant to: `https://customer-redirect-url.com/call-completed`

**‼️ Note** If you also need to pass participant or session metadata to the redirected page, see [Send Metadata via URL Parameters](https://www.strella.io/education-hub/tutorial/send-metadata-via-url-parameters) .

## **Common Mistakes**

- Entering a partial URL instead of a full absolute URL (e.g. without `https://)`‍
- Saving the setting in the wrong project
