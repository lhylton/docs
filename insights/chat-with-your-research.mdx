AI Chat is one of Strella's most powerful features. Think of it as a built-in ChatGPT that knows everything your participants said — you can ask it questions, uncover patterns, generate reports, and save the results as highlight reels, all without leaving your project.

## Access AI Chat

In the top navigation bar of any project page, click **AI Chat** to open the chat interface. AI Chat has two modes: **Find Insights** and **Create a Report**.

<Tip>
  AI Chat is available on every project page. You don't need to navigate away from your current view — just click the button in the top nav bar.
</Tip>

---

## The two AI Chat modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Find Insights">
    Use this mode to ask open-ended questions about your research and let AI search across all participant sessions to pull out relevant answers.

    ### Ask a question

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select 'Find Insights' mode">
        Make sure the **Find Insights** tab is active in the AI Chat interface. This is the default mode when you open chat.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type your question and send it">
        Ask anything related to your research — for example, *"What frustrations did participants mention about the onboarding experience?"* or *"Were there any moments of surprise or delight?"*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review the response and participant quotes">
        AI Chat returns a synthesized response. When the answer includes a participant quote, you'll see a **circular icon with the participant's initials** next to the relevant text. Click the icon to expand the exact quote, then click the quote itself to jump directly to that moment in the participant's session.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ### Save an insight

    When AI Chat returns a response that includes participant quotes, a **Save insight** button appears at the bottom of that message. Click it to save the insight to your **Saved tab**, where Strella automatically generates an associated highlight reel.

    See [Creating Highlight Reels](/insights/creating-highlight-reels) and [Insights Board](/insights/insights-board) for more on what happens after you save.

    ### Find insights across multiple projects

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open AI Chat and look for the project selector">
        In the chat interface, locate the option to select which projects to include in the search.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select multiple projects">
        Choose two or more projects to combine their participant data in a single conversation. This is particularly useful when a study has been split across multiple Strella projects and you want to identify themes that span all of them.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Ask cross-study questions">
        Pose questions as you normally would. AI Chat searches across all selected projects simultaneously and surfaces patterns consistent across participant groups.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      Cross-project chat is especially valuable for longitudinal studies, multi-cohort research, or when comparing responses from different participant segments run as separate projects.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Create a Report">
    Use this mode to generate a polished, structured report from your participant data — ready to share with stakeholders.

    ### Generate a report

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select 'Create a Report' mode">
        In the AI Chat interface, click the **Create a Report** tab to switch modes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Describe the report you want">
        Tell AI Chat what kind of report you need. Be as specific as helpful — for example, *"Create an executive summary of key usability findings with supporting quotes"* or *"Generate a report on how participants responded to the new pricing page."*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review the generated report">
        AI Chat searches across all sessions in your selected project, pulls in relevant insights, themes, and participant responses, and produces a tailored report. Read through it and identify anything you'd like to refine.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Continue chatting to refine the report">
        Reply in the chat to adjust the report — ask for more detail on a specific section, a different tone, additional participant quotes, or a shorter summary. AI Chat updates the report iteratively.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save and share the report">
        Once you're satisfied, save the report. For full details on formatting options and sharing reports with stakeholders, see the [Navigating Reports](/results/navigating-reports) page.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Start with a broad prompt to get an initial structure, then follow up with targeted requests to add depth to specific sections. Treating it like a conversation produces better results than trying to specify everything in a single message.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
